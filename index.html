01Overview of Application Functions

Numora Calc is a multifunctional toolkit designed to provide standard calculators, specialty calculation modules (including Age Calculator, Unit Converter, and Discount calculators), real-time Currency Conversion tools, a local Notes Manager (Sticky Logs), and Menstrual Cycle tracking & prediction tools.

02Information We Collect and How We Handle It

To support these utilities, Numora Calc is structured to run primarily offline, using sandboxed local storage on your device. We prioritize data minimization and do not run continuous tracking scripts or background telemetry.

A. User-Authored Content & Tool Calculations (Stored Locally Only)

Users may voluntarily enter data into our specialized calculating modules. This includes:

  • Numerical entries, equations, birthdates, and discount percentages.
  • Custom text notes, Sticky Pad entries, and calculation logs.
  • Menstrual Cycle & Health Tracking Parameters: last period start dates, cycle lengths, period durations, and customized cycle logs.
Storage commitment: All calculation inputs, notes, and cycle parameters are saved exclusively on your device within the app's sandboxed environment (client-side local storage). This sensitive information is never automatically synchronized to external servers, sold, or shared with third parties by Blue Acid Labs.

B. License Registration & Verification Data (Account Information)

To access and verify your perpetual standard platform license, we collect minimal operational info during sign-up:

  • Email address — used solely for license binding, service communication, and activation checks.
  • Country selection — collected to adapt default currency formats and comply with regional tax guidelines.
  • Billing details — handled exclusively by our secure third-party payment gateway. Blue Acid Labs does not store or process your credit card numbers or raw bank credentials.

C. Network Connection Data

  • Live exchange rates — when you launch the Currency Converter module, the app sends a secure, non-identifying API request to retrieve live exchange rates. No personal information or browser-identifying metadata is transmitted with this request.
  • License checks — a simple online validation verifies your standard account status.

D. Diagnostics & Performance Analytics

To ensure stability, eliminate app crashes, and deliver performance optimizations, we may gather anonymous device statistics:

  • Operating system version (e.g., Android, iOS).
  • Device model configuration.
  • Anonymous diagnostic logs and crash reports.

03Menstrual Cycle & Health-Related Estimations

  • Informational estimations only: calculations in the Menstrual Cycle module (including next period forecasts, ovulation predictions, and fertile window estimations) are generated via client-side algorithms based solely on your user-inputted cycle length.
  • No medical reliance: this module is provided for self-organization and personal informational purposes only. It should not be treated as a form of contraception, medical diagnosis, or healthcare advice. Consult a qualified medical practitioner for all medical needs.

04Application Permissions and System Access

Numora Calc requests the absolute minimum system permissions required to function, following the principle of least privilege:

  • Network access (INTERNET) — used only to update currency exchange rate tables and verify active standard licenses.
  • Local sandboxed storage — reads and writes to the secure client-side database on your device to keep notes, calculation history, and presets safe.
  • Zero dangerous permissions requested — the application does NOT request access to your Location, Camera, Contacts, SMS, Files, or Microphone.

05Third-Party Services

We may integrate highly verified standard services to facilitate core operations. These processors may process technical metrics in accordance with their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services (for app distribution and billing integration)
  • Firebase / Google Analytics (for crashlytics and stability diagnostics)
  • Payment Gateways (to manage secure licensing charges)

06Children's Privacy

Numora Calc does not knowingly collect, request, or target personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a minor has registered an email account with us, please contact us immediately so we can remove the account registry.

07Data Storage, Security, and Your Deletion Rights

  • Data security: we implement standard operational and logical safeguards to protect the integrity of verified email registrations and account transactions.
  • On-demand instant deletion (the Purge Right): we fully respect your right to data erasure under Google Play Store policy, GDPR, and CCPA. Users can permanently delete all stored data, custom cycle notes, math histories, and clear system licensing associations instantly by clicking the "Delete All Stored Data" option inside the App's User Settings panel.

08Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy to match potential feature updates. If we introduce cloud synchronization features in the future, we will update this policy and request your explicit opt-in confirmation within the app beforehand.
